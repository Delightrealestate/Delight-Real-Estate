Our Policies & Process

Transparent and structured approach to all our real estate transactions and consultancy services.

Deal Protocol & Structure

Every client engagement begins with a structured protocol. We assign a dedicated lead advisor, establish clear communication channels, and define a step-by-step roadmap from initial due diligence to final closing.

Consultancy Fee Structure

Our consultancy fee is typically structured as a retainer or a fixed project fee, agreed upon upfront. This covers our deep-dive research, market analysis, and strategic advisory services.

Commission Policy

Upon the successful execution of a transaction (Buy/Sale/Lease), a commission is earned. This is a pre-negotiated percentage of the transaction value or a success fee, detailed explicitly in our engagement agreement.
